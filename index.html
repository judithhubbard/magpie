Magpie

A lightweight image search across free & Creative Commons sources.

Magpie searches across many open image collections — Wikimedia, Library of Congress, Internet Archive, museum collections, scientific specimen databases, stock photography, and more — and shows the results with proper attribution and license info for each image.

Each image carries its own license, displayed in the lightbox. Verify the license before using an image.
